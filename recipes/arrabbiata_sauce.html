Arrabbiata Sauce

Description

Arrabbiata sauce is spicy and delicious. Ideal on penne pasta.

Ingredients

  • 1 teasponn olive oil
  • 1 cup chopped onion
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 cans peeled and diced tomatoes
  • 3/8 cup red wine
  • 2 tablespoons tomato paste
  • 1 tablespoon white sugar
  • 1 tablespoon chopped fresh basil
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • 1 teaspoon crushed red pepper flakes
  • 1/2 teaspoon Italian seasoning
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 2 tablespoonschopped fresh parsley

Steps

  1. Heat oil in a large skillet or saucepan over medium heat. Add onion and garlic; cook and stir until softened, about 5 minutes.
  2. Stir in diced tomatoes, wine, tomato paste, sugar, basil, lemon juice, red pepper flakes, Italian seasoning, and black pepper; bring to a boil. Reduce heat to medium and simmer, uncovered, for about 15 minutes.
  3. Stir in parsley. Ladle sauce over hot cooked pasta of your choice.
  4. Serve hot and enjoy!
